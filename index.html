The idea

You are British Columbia. The province starts at the back of the pack and you are pulling it to the front. Every day you hold your goal, BC charters its next real city, in the order those cities actually joined the province, starting with New Westminster in 1860. Reach 155 strong days and BC sits at number one.

Each day

1
Held it today? Tap Stayed strong today. A new city lights up on the map and your streak goes up by one.
2
Slipped? Tap Log a slip. You lose 3 points and the streak goes back to zero. The cities you already built stay where they are. Nothing on the map is taken away.
3
Forgot a day? Open the Monthly tab and tap any past day to set it. You can backfill or fix anything.

Points

Points come from streak length. The longer you go without a reset, the bigger the reward when you cross each mark.

If you reset and climb back, you earn those streak bonuses again. Rebuilding counts.

The map

British Columbia is divided into its census regions — the same regional districts the province really uses. Each region fills in from sea-blue toward gold as you charter the cities inside it, so the map is a picture of your progress. Tap any region to see its cities: the ones you have already founded and the ones still waiting, each with the year it was incorporated. Use + and − to zoom, drag to move around, and Aa to show region names.

Tracking

The Daily tab shows your current streak and the next milestone. Weekly shows this week plus the last twelve. Monthly is your calendar and your edit screen. City Atlas lists all 160 municipalities in the order they were founded, so you can see exactly which city each strong day unlocks next.

One honest note

A slip is one day. It does not erase the run that came before it, and it does not decide the next one. The map keeps every city you earned. Mark the day, and pick it back up tomorrow.
